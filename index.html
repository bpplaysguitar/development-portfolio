My name is Brian Palay, and I'm a web developer. More than that, I'm a creative mind. I've been writing and playing music for most of my life, having been a part of the music communities in Minneapolis and Nashville. Music has been keeping my creativity active my whole life.

I graduated from the University of Minnesota with a Bachelor of Arts in architecture. My work after that includes many years in the e-commerce content administration space, which saw me regularly working with product photography, graphic design, copy writing and SEO, and 3D modeling and rendering of products and retail spaces. I have since found new motivation and excitement applying my creativity and eye for detail to the development world.
